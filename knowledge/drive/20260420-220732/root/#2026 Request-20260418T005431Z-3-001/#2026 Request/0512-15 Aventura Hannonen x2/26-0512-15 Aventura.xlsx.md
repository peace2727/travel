Next, take a stroll around Bukchon Hanok Village, which consists of about 900 traditional homes called "hanok." Continue on to Insadong Street, filled with antiques and traditional crafts & goods. Then explore Gwangjang Market, which offers a vibrant mix of traditional Korean street food and bustling stalls. At the market, try the mung bean pancakes and other snacks.
