| Seoul Through a Creative Lens Tour
Enjoy a full day tour of trendy Seoul.
Start the day immersed in Korea’s modern art scene at the National Museum of Modern and Contemporary Art, Seoul (MMCA Seoul), where innovative works by Korean and international artists come to life in a striking contemporary space.
Then move into one of Seoul’s most dynamic neighborhoods, Euljiro, where old industrial streets meet a new wave of creativity, earning it the nickname “Hipjiro” for its trendy transformation.
With several photobooth studios nearby, like DON'T LXXK UP, you will also have the opportunity to capture fun, uniquely Korean-style photo memories.
Conclude at the iconic Dongdaemun Design Plaza (DDP), a futuristic landmark where cutting-edge design, exhibitions, and vibrant city energy come together.

| Seoul Trendsetter Experience Tour
Enjoy a half day tour of chic Seoul.
Transfer and meet with your guide to start your adventure in the vibrant district of Seongsu-dong, where old factories have been reimagined into stylish cafés and creative concept stores. The area is known for its raw industrial charm blended with modern design, offering a lively atmosphere filled with local brands, experimental concepts, and ever-changing trends.
Then head to the fashionable areas of Apgujeong-dong and Garosu-gil, where tree-lined streets are lined with trendy boutiques, designer labels, and chic cafés. Enjoy leisurely shopping and soak in Seoul’s contemporary lifestyle scene.
At the end of the tour, you will have free time to continue exploring the area.

Oct 8 (Thu) BUSAN |  |  |  |  |  |  |  |  |  |
|  |  |  |  |  |  |  |  |  |  |
| Coastal Busan Tour
Begin your day at the colorful hillside community of Gamcheon Culture Village. Wander through its maze of narrow alleys, vibrant murals, and whimsical installations. Here you have the opportunity to enjoy hands-on DIY art experiences where you can create your own postcards, crafts, or painted souvenirs inspired by the village’s colorful surroundings.
Next, travel across the city to F1963 Cultural Complex, a beautifully repurposed industrial space that once produced wire rods and now serves as one of Busan’s trendiest cultural hubs. Explore modern art exhibitions, design bookstores, stylish cafés, and curated lifestyle shops..
In the afternoon, head toward the coast and arrive at Haeundae Beach, Korea’s most famous beach. Take time to stroll along the shoreline, enjoy the sea breeze, and soak in the lively yet relaxed atmosphere of this iconic destination.
Conclude at Haeundae Blueline Park (nearby Mipo Harbor), boarding the scenic beach train for a sunset ride along the coastline, offering breathtaking ocean views and a memorable, picture-perfect ending to the day.
Return to the hotel and rest at leisure. |  |  |  |  |  |  |  |  |  |
