| A Journey Through Seoul’s Past and Present
Experience the perfect blend of tradition and modern life on a full-day tour of Seoul.
Start the day with a relaxing stroll along Cheonggyecheon Stream, a peaceful urban oasis flowing through downtown Seoul, where walking paths, bridges, and gentle water features offer a refreshing contrast to the bustling city above.
Head over to Gwangjang Market and explore one of Seoul's famous street food markets. 
Next, stroll through the charming alleyways of Bukchon Hanok Village, where hundreds of well-preserved traditional Korean houses (hanok) sit on gentle hills between modern neighborhoods, offering a glimpse into Seoul’s past. 
Continue on to Insadong, a cultural district known for its antique shops, art galleries, traditional tea houses, and souvenir stores showcasing Korean craftsmanship. 
Just nearby is Ikseondong, a trendy yet nostalgic area where renovated hanok buildings house stylish cafés, boutique shops, and unique eateries, blending old-world charm with modern creativity. 
Then, return to the hotel and rest at leisure. |  |  |  |  |  |  |  |  |  |
