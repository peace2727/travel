| Coastal Busan Tour
Begin your day at the colorful hillside community of Gamcheon Culture Village. Wander through its maze of narrow alleys, vibrant murals, and whimsical installations. Here you have the opportunity to enjoy hands-on DIY art experiences where you can create your own postcards, crafts, or painted souvenirs inspired by the village’s colorful surroundings.
Next, travel across the city to F1963 Cultural Complex, a beautifully repurposed industrial space that once produced wire rods and now serves as one of Busan’s trendiest cultural hubs. Explore modern art exhibitions, design bookstores, stylish cafés, and curated lifestyle shops..
In the afternoon, head toward the coast and arrive at Haeundae Beach, Korea’s most famous beach. Take time to stroll along the shoreline, enjoy the sea breeze, and soak in the lively yet relaxed atmosphere of this iconic destination.
Conclude at Haeundae Blueline Park (nearby Mipo Harbor), boarding the scenic beach train for a sunset ride along the coastline, offering breathtaking ocean views and a memorable, picture-perfect ending to the day.
Return to the hotel and rest at leisure. |  |  |  |  |  |  |  |  |  |
